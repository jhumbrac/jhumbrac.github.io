Opening Spring 2023

At the turn of the 20th century, farms gave way to roads as the Western Business District was developed along Charlotte Pike. Among these new buildings stood Lovell, Gower, and Co Grocery, whose owners chose the location because of the nearby streetcar stop. More than 100 years later, Streetcar Taps and Garden will open its doors to provide a place to gather between Sylvan Park and the Nations. Chef Carter Hach

Under the direction of Chef Carter Hach, Streetcar Taps and Garden serves up seasonal menus, beer, wine, and cocktails in a turn of the century brick building that once housed West Nashville's first grocery. Locally owned and operated, Streetcar appreciates the rich history of Sylvan Park, Charlotte Pike, and the Nations.
